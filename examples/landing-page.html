Validate

Nothing moves before the checks pass

Identity, balance, dispute history, and contact permissions are confirmed at intake. An account that fails a check holds in place with the reason attached, rather than advancing.

Outreach

Contact inside the rules that apply

Channel, timing, and frequency follow the rule set for the account and the jurisdiction. The active window and the chosen channel are visible on the record, so a reviewer can see the why, not just the what.

Resolution

Every outcome is documented

Arrangements, disputes, and closures write back to the account with a timestamp and an actor. Reporting reads from that same trail, so monthly results reconcile to individual account histories.
