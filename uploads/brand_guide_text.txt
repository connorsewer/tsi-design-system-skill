TSI is the consumer credit industry’s technology and customer-centric leader in
receivables optimization and servicing customer relationships.
TSI works for its clients in six key ways:
• 	It recovers debt while preserving customer relationships, protecting the long-term
value of those relationships.
• 	It’s vigorously compliant with consumer credit regulations, ensuring its clients avoid
fines and damage to their brands and reputations.
• 	Its proprietary analytics improve both efficiency of its teams and the results they
achieve in compliance with laws and regulations.
• 	It uses omnichannel communications to reach debtors in the way they prefer,
decreasing friction and increasing recoveries.
• 	Its customer-centric culture fuels client satisfaction and loyalty.
• 	Its vertically organized teams bring deep domain expertise to solving client problems.
Positioning
(the target audience for the one-to-many conversation)
For… senior leaders and mid-level managers responsible for managing customer
relationships, accounts receivables and loan servicing for consumer and medical debt.
(their problem)
Who… are challenged to service loans, provide customer service and increase recoveries
while minimizing compliance risk; and who need to preserve long-term customer
relationships and the reputation of their brands.
(how you solve it)
TSI… provides customers with timely and accurate reporting, omnichannel communications,
and the advanced and proprietary analytics necessary to service customers and recover debt;
it supports customers with a highly responsive team focused on customer success; and
scalable outsourced support with deep expertise in vertical industries including consumer
loans and healthcare.
(toward separation)
Unlike… loan servicers, collection agencies or customer service teams that lack advanced
data mining and analytics technologies, risk compliance violations with shoddy practices
and use outsourcing for labor arbitrage.
(why customers should care)
Only TSI… is the credit, loan and customer service industries’ customer-first provider.
Powered by advanced analytics and omnichannel communications technology and backed
by a team laser focused on your success, it protects your brand and the relationship with
your customer, while recovering high levels of consumer and medical debt.

It’s time for new thinking about customer relationships and credit
It’s hard to acquire a new customer, and it’s costly to lose them. Which is why many leading
brands focus on the lifetime value of - and a lifetime relationship with - their customer or
patient.
They want positive interactions with customers in order to preserve the opportunity to sell to
or serve that customer in the future. They know providing top quality, relationship-oriented
service improves their ability to sustain the customer relationship, even when conversations
are difficult and oriented toward recovering debt.
And they know running afoul of increasingly stringent consumer credit regulations can result
in fines and damage to reputation and brand.
Navigating this minefield requires a different kind of partner. It’s why so many top brands
trust TSI.
They’ve seen how TSI makes their problems, its problems. How it anticipates issues rather
than waiting on instructions. And they’ve seen that customer-first isn’t a slogan but rather a
core value baked into the way TSI does business.
TSI’s omnichannel communications technology and proprietary analytics fuel the efficiency
of its teams while improving customer experiences and client outcomes.
TSI achieves superior results too with teams organized around key verticals and possessing
deep industry domain expertise. Because the challenges of recovering medical debt are
different, and require different approaches, than servicing a utility customer, or recovering
credit card debt.
TSI is unique in the world of credit. Its technology, analytics, culture, vertical domain
expertise — and the results it delivers on behalf of its clients — separate it from every other
company in its field.

Compelling logos are designed
to communicate a core idea and
visually represent the essence of

TSI logo meaning
At the heart of the TSI logo
is the TSI symbol. It represents
trust, innovation, and a platform
for recovery optimization.
The elevated square suggests
out-of-the-box innovation and
an elevated, customer first

an established and relevant
company that is reliable,
professional, and relevant
to today’s contemporary
business challenges.

Color palette
Following are guidelines and
specifications for the TSI brand
color palette.
Color use
Use TSI dark blue, bright blue, a
10-20% screen tint of bright blue,
and white as the dominant colors
throughout communications.
Only use the secondary green and
orange as an accent for
applications such as graphs,
charts, web buttons,
and other elements needing
differentiation. Screen tints of the
colors may also be used.
Color modes
Color specifications are different
across various media. Therefore, it
is important to use the correct
color mode for the specific media
application to achieve the
intended colors.
Web & Microsoft applications
Web and Microsoft applications
use the RGB color mode.
Video applications
Video applications use the HEX
color mode.
Digital printing
Digital printing from Adobe
programs such as Adobe Illustrator
and Adobe InDesign use the CMYK
color mode.
Spot, screen printing,
and embroidery
Spot, screen printing, and
embroidery use Pantone colors.

emphasis or correct grammar
call-outs, and not as a dominant
design expression throughout
the communication application.

Calibri may be used as an
